Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These classic chocolate chip cookies are the perfect balance of crispy edges and soft, chewy centers. Packed with generous handfuls of semi-sweet chocolate chips, every bite delivers a sweet, comforting flavor that's sure to satisfy any craving. The dough is easy to make, and the result is a batch of irresistible cookies that fill your kitchen with a warm, mouthwatering aroma.

With just the right amount of sweetness and a hint of vanilla, these cookies are a timeless treat. Whether you're enjoying them with a glass of milk, sharing them with friends, or snacking on them all by yourself, this simple recipe will quickly become a favorite in your baking repertoire.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup margarine
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 cups of milk chocolate

Directions
