Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a classic comfort food that never goes out of style. With its moist, sweet, and slightly spiced flavor, it’s the perfect treat to enjoy with a cup of coffee or as a quick snack. This versatile bread is made with ripe bananas, which bring natural sweetness and a soft texture, making it a favorite for bakers of all levels. Whether you’re looking to use up overripe bananas or just craving a delicious homemade snack, banana bread is always a reliable choice.

What makes banana bread even more appealing is how easy it is to make. With just a handful of ingredients and a few simple steps, you can whip up a loaf that fills your kitchen with mouthwatering aromas. From the basic recipe to endless variations with add-ins like chocolate chips, nuts, or spices, banana bread is endlessly customizable. This comforting bake is not only a crowd-pleaser but also a great way to show off your baking skills, even if you’re a beginner in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 2 well beaten eggs
  • 4 tablespoons sour milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 1 cup banana pulp
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups flour

Directions

  1. Mix shortening, sugar, eggs, banana, sour milk, baking soda, and vanilla.
  2. Mix in flour and add nuts (optional).
  3. Pour into a greased and floured pan.
  4. Bake for 60 minutes at 350 degrees.
